About Us

Hello everyone! We are HBM-Logistics, a Small Enterprise created by 3 students from Institut Cirvianum de Torello.

We decided to create this Enterprise due to the fact that we live in a county which feedstock is widely produced, consumed and transported, so we saw a huge opportunity to try to get in to this work field and start our own transportation Enteprise.

In the beginning we'll mainly transport throughout the region, but if everything goes as planned we'll go international and trasport the amazing goods from our county, Osona, all over the world!

Thank you for your attention, I really hope you see potential in our Enterprise and motivate us to go further!
